# Call Helper App

Call Helper is an Android app that lets you schedule phone calls at a specific time and date. 

### Key Features
* Schedule calls to any contact at a chosen time.
* 20-second warning alert with a ringtone before the call starts.
* Option to snooze or cancel the call during the warning.
* Works even when the screen is locked or turned off.
* Dark mode and Light mode support.
* Call history log to track completed and cancelled calls.
* Recurring call support for daily reminders.

### How it works
1. Set the contact, date, and time.
2. When the time comes, a full-screen alert will pop up.
3. You can choose to call immediately, snooze for 5 minutes, or cancel.
4. If no action is taken, the app will automatically dial the number after 20 seconds.

### Permissions
The app needs the following permissions to work correctly:
* CALL_PHONE: To make the actual phone call.
* READ_CONTACTS: To pick a number from your contact list.
* SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: To trigger the call at the exact time.
* POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To show alerts.
* Display over other apps: To show the countdown while the screen is on.

### Use of this application
### Use Cases
* Daily Family Check-ins**: Schedule a recurring call to your parents or 
    loved ones every evening so you never forget to stay connected.
* Professional Meetings**: Set calls for important business appointments or
    interviews. The 20-second warning gives you time to find a quiet place and 
    grab your notes before the call starts.
* International Calls**: If you need to call someone in a different time zone, 
    schedule it in advance so you don't miss the specific window when they are available.
* Medicine or Health Reminders**: Use it to call a doctor or health service at a 
    specific time, or to check on a friend who is recovering.
* Elderly Support**: Helps seniors who might forget to make important calls. Once set
    by a family member, the phone will automatically alert them and dial.
* Task Management**: Perfect for service calls (like calling your mechanic or plumber)
    that need to happen during specific business hours while you are busy working.
